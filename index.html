Finding the best car insurance
company for you

Pricing and discounts made up a portion of each insurer’s
score, but we know that cost is the top factor for many auto
insurance shoppers. Since pricing is based on personal
factors including your age, location, vehicle and credit
history and gender (in most cases), it’s important to shop
around for car insurance quotes.

If price is the most important factor in your search,
comparing car insurance quotes can help you find the
cheapest rate. But your shopping experience will also shed
light on a company’s customer service standards.

To find the top car insurance companies:

  1. Visit your state insurance commissioner’s website to find
    complaints and other information about companies in your area.
  2. Ask your agent or a customer service representative any
    questions you have to make sure you’re getting all the coverage
    you need — and nothing you don’t want.
  3. Consider smaller insurance companies, too. Small companies
    offer rates well below large competitors in many locations.
  4. If you’ve had tickets, DUIs or other violations, be sure to shop
    three and five years after the incident, when surcharges can drop
    significantly.
